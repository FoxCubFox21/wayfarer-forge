03 / MANIFESTO
Wayfarer Forge builds small, quiet games about strangers who pass through — lost astronauts dropped into other peoples' stories, forced to listen, then forced to choose.
· · · · ·
No combat. No signup. No account. No ads. No DLC. No tutorial. Just open a page and walk.

Every entry in the series shares one recurring protagonist and one loop: arrive, listen, choose, leave. Every choice in every game is an ending — two or more, always bittersweet, always yours to live with.
