ABOUT
Wayfarer Forge is a one-person studio making small, quiet games about strangers who pass through. Each entry shares one recurring protagonist — the astronaut you'll meet in The Wounded, Never Wolves — and a single loop: arrive, listen, choose, leave.

No signup, no account, no ads. Just open a page and walk.
